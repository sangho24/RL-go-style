Bingsheng Cup

The Bingsheng Cup (穹窿山兵聖杯, Qionglong Mountain Bingsheng Cup) was an international Go championship for women, held ten times, from 2010 to 2019, playing under Chinese rules. The time limit was 2h. Komi was 7.5.
