Honinbo Shuhaku

Honinbo Shuhaku (本因坊秀伯, 1716–1741) was the 7th Honinbo (from 1733). A twenty-game match (sogo) was to be fought between Shuhaku and Shunseki, the 6th head of the Inoue house. In the first 8 games the score was 4 wins, 3 losses, 1 jigo (with Shuhaku taking B 2 out of every 3 games). In the 9th game, Shuhaku vomited blood and the series was interrupted. Shuhaku dies on 1741-03-27.

Here we have two early games with Aihara Kaseki, 7 Castle Games, and 8 games from his match with Inoue Shunseki (where the last Castle Game is simultaneously the first game of this match).
