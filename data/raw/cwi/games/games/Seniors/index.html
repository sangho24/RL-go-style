1st Seniors Tournament

There has been only a single edition of the Seniors Go Tournament (シニア棋戦). It had 13 participants and was held between October 1984 and April 1985 with a Judan-like setup.

The final between Hoshino Toshi and Sakai Michiharu was won by the former.
