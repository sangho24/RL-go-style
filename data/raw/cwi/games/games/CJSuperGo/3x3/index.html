Japan-China NEC Supergo

After the crushing defeat of Japan in 1996, the setup of the Japan-China Supergo matches was changed.

In the new system, three players from China and three players from Japan battle. The chosen players are the winners of the NEC Cups, of the NEC Young Stars tournaments, and of the Women's Kakusei in each country. Each plays a best-of-three match with the corresponding winner from the other country.

The Supergo numbering is continued at 12. (We follow the Kido Yb.)
